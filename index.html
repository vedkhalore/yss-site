About Yuva Shakti Sangathan

A youth movement dedicated to skills, safety, health, market access and environmental action � designed to be school-friendly with clear schemes and practice Q&A. We combine grassroots action with scalable programs to impact the lives of young Indians.

Our Vision & Official Declaration

This is the official website of a political party Yuva Shakti Sangathan. We think that India�s youth are not just the leaders of tomorrow � they are the force of today. We aim to empower, equip, and unite them for collective progress.

We the Yuva Shakti Sangathan ensure

  • To empower every youth with skills, dignity and opportunities.
  • To create a society where safety, equality and respect for women are non-negotiable.
  • To promote innovation in education and bridge the digital divide.
  • To strengthen local economies and help Indian products reach the world.
  • To protect our environment through active youth participation.
  • To ensure transparent governance through citizen driven policy feedback systems.

Flagship Schemes (introduced to make these dreams true)

We hope that together we can build a safer, healthier, greener tomorrow. If you think we have the potential to change a bit of our country's development graph so please vote for us in the upcoming election.

Flagship Schemes � Detailed

Mission Skill 360

1-year paid National Skill Fellowship � ?15,000/month while working on community, tech and startup projects.

  • Skill Labs in every district with local industries.
  • Startup stamp-duty waiver for founders under 30.
  • Mentorship, stipend, placement support.

Sakhi Suraksha Grid

GPS safe-route app, live police patrol tracking, and instant distress video calling.

  • Paid 'Return to Work' Fellowship for women with re-training.
  • Community-verified safe points and volunteer rapid-response.

Healthy Bharat Card

AI-based predictive health check once a year � free � to detect early risks.

  • Youth Health Corps: mental health awareness, first aid & screening.
  • Integration with local clinics for follow-up referrals.

Local to Global Mission

Export Support Cells for artisans, farmers and MSMEs � branding, digital marketing and international links.

  • 'Market Without Middlemen' � bi-monthly direct sales in cities.
  • Training in packaging, quality standards & export docs.

Digital Pehla Kadam

Innovation corners in every school (incl. govt schools) with 3D printers, robotics & coding resources.

  • 'Earn While You Learn' internships for Classes 11�12.
  • Teacher training for sustainable maintenance.

Green Guardian Program

Youth-led teams monitor air & water quality with open public dashboards.

  • Paid internships for planting & maintaining urban micro-forests.
  • Open data to drive civic engagement & transparency.
